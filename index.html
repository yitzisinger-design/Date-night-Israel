Tel Aviv

The city that never sleeps — vibrant nightlife, world-class chefs, Mediterranean soul.

Restaurant

Mashya

Modern Israeli / Mediterranean
Elegant Levantine cuisine inside the historic Hotel Montefiore. Seasonal tasting menus highlight local produce with French technique.
Best: Thu-Sat, 8-10 PM $$$ Neve Tzedek
Bar

Imperial Craft

Cocktail Bar
Award-winning cocktails in a glamorous art-deco space on HaYarkon. Bartenders tailor drinks to your mood. A Tel Aviv institution.
Best: Thu-Fri, 9 PM-midnight $$-$$$ HaYarkon St
Restaurant

Claro

Mediterranean / Wood-Fire
Chef Ran Shmueli's wood-fire grill in Jaffa Port. Whole fish, seasonal vegetables, and house-made pastas in a stunning stone setting.
Best: Fri lunch or Sat, 8 PM $$$ Jaffa Port
Wine Bar

Jaja

Natural Wine Bar
Intimate natural wine bar on Rabbi Meir St. Israeli and European bottles, cheese boards, and a cozy candlelit atmosphere.
Best: Tue-Wed, 7-10 PM $$ Lev Ha'Ir
Cocktails

Bellboy

Speakeasy
Hidden behind an unmarked door, this speakeasy serves inventive cocktails with Middle Eastern botanicals. Ring the bell to enter.
Best: Thu-Sat, 10 PM+ $$-$$$ Lilienblum St
Rooftop

Sachi

Pan-Asian / Rooftop
Rooftop dining atop the Norman hotel with panoramic sea views. Pan-Asian flavors, creative sushi, and sunset cocktails.
Best: Sunset, any evening $$$-$$$$ Nachmani St

Jerusalem

Ancient stone meets modern flavor — a city of depth, soul, and surprising culinary creativity.

Restaurant

Machneyuda

Market-to-Table
The legendary shuk restaurant. Loud, joyful, and bursting with flavor. Chefs cook in an open kitchen to pounding music. Iconic.
Best: Tue-Thu, 8:30 PM $$$ Mahane Yehuda
Bar

BeerBazaar

Craft Beer Bar
Over 100 Israeli craft beers on tap inside the Mahane Yehuda market. After the stalls close, the shuk transforms into bar alley.
Best: Thu night, 9 PM+ $-$$ Mahane Yehuda
Restaurant

Satya

Modern Israeli
From the Machneyuda team — a slightly more intimate setting with bold flavors and a creative cocktail menu to match.
Best: Mon-Wed, 8 PM $$$ Mahane Yehuda
Wine Bar

Rooftop at Mamilla

Wine & Views
Sip Israeli wines overlooking the Old City walls. An unbeatable backdrop for a romantic evening with curated wine flights.
Best: Sunset, Sun-Thu $$$ Mamilla

Haifa

A chill port city with a rising food scene — coexistence on a plate, views of the bay.

Restaurant

Fattoush

Arab-Israeli / Fine Dining
Elevated Arab cuisine in the German Colony. Chef Alaa Musa creates stunning dishes rooted in local tradition with modern presentation.
Best: Fri-Sat, 8 PM $$$ German Colony
Bar

Libira

Craft Brewery & Bar
Haifa's beloved craft brewery in the port area. Fresh house brews, pub food, and a relaxed vibe with harbor views.
Best: Thu-Sat, 7 PM $$ Downtown Port
Rooftop

Etz Cafe

Cafe-Bar / Views
Perched on the Carmel ridge with sweeping bay views. Great cocktails, light bites, and one of the best sunset spots in the north.
Best: Sunset, any evening $$ Carmel Center

The Galilee

Wine country, boutique farms, and lake-side dining — Israel's romantic north.

Wine Bar

Tulip Winery

Winery & Tasting Room
Award-winning boutique winery in Kfar Tikva. Book a private tasting with cheese pairings overlooking rolling Galilean hills.
Best: Fri afternoon, 2-5 PM $$ Kfar Tikva
Restaurant

Magdalena

Galilean Farm-to-Table
On the shores of the Sea of Galilee at the Scots Hotel. Locally sourced ingredients, fresh fish, and a serene lakeside setting.
Best: Sat evening, 7 PM $$$ Tiberias
Restaurant

HaBait Shel Ramzi

Druze Home Cooking
Authentic Druze home restaurant in Daliat el-Carmel. Multi-course feasts of handmade dishes served family-style. Book ahead.
Best: Sat lunch, 12-2 PM $$ Daliat el-Carmel

Eilat & The Negev

Desert heat, Red Sea breezes — tropical cocktails and fresh seafood at the southern tip.

Restaurant

Pago Pago

Seafood
Fresh Red Sea fish and seafood right on the marina. Grilled calamari, shrimp, and catch of the day with views of the water.
Best: Any evening, 7-9 PM $$-$$$ North Beach Marina
Cocktails

Three Monkeys

Tropical Cocktail Bar
Tiki-style bar on the promenade. Frozen cocktails, rum punches, and a party atmosphere with the Red Sea mountains as backdrop.
Best: Fri-Sat, 9 PM+ $$ Promenade
